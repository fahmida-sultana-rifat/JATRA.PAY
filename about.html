About JATRA.PAY

JATRA.PAY is a fast, secure, and reliable digital payment platform built to help drivers and passengers make small payments instantly — no cash, no waiting.

Our Mission

To create a seamless cashless experience for public transport payments through innovation and trust.

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh’s leading digital platform for small-scale driver and commuter transactions.

Why Choose JATRA.PAY?

Multiple Payment Options

From bKash and Nagad to SIM recharge, choose your preferred way to pay.

1-Click QR Payments

Fast and easy payments through our QR scanner technology.

Safe & Secure

End-to-end encrypted transactions to protect every user.
